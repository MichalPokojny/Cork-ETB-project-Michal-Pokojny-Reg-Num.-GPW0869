Best restaurants in Dublin

1. Chapter One

Five Star Chapter One restaurant is located in Dublin city close to theatres and shopping areas. This innovative restaurant has a long-standing history on the Michelin Guide list for its remarkable dining experiences as well as pleasing your palette with the food.

Type of Cuisine: Irish / European / Contemporary / Modern Cuisine

2. The Marker Brasserie

The Brasserie at the Marker Hotel incorporates a relaxed dining experience in a space of elemental light and energy that transforms seamlessly from day to night. The Brasserie is a destination for food lovers who love to try something different, yet share a genuine passion for discovering simple, unfussy dishes made with locally sourced ingredients.

Type of Cuisine: Irish / European / International

3. Locks

Introducing Locks Windsor Terrace, bringing the best in modern Irish food to a much-loved venue in Dublin 8. The goal here is to warmly welcome our guests into one of Dublin's best dining rooms with an exciting and constantly evolving menu, extensive wine & cocktail list, great music and a laid-back atmosphere.

Type of Cuisine: Irish / Contemporary / Modern Cuisine

4. The Saddle Room

This serene hotel restaurant serves a classy and opulent feel from the get-go. It is named after The Shelbourne Hotel's historic dining rooms and is an old favourite reinvented as a Dublin steakhouse and seafood restaurant serving breakfast, lunch and dinner. The Saddle Room Restaurant has two private dining rooms, available for private hire for lunch or dinner.

Type of Cuisine: Irish / International / European

5. Seasons

Seasons Restaurant at the Intercontinental Hotel celebrates contemporary Irish cuisine, lovingly prepared by Executive Chef Alberto Rossi and his expert Five Star team, Seasons Restaurant uses only the finest seasonal ingredients to present you with 'artwork on a plate!'

Type of Cuisine: Irish / International / European
